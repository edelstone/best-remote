About this project

Overview

Why I made this

I've been doing a lot of research on remote companies for my next job opportunity, and I think it might help others who are interested in remote work. There are lots of good resources out there on remote jobs, but none that focus specifically on gathering the most interesting information (recruitment sites, articles, blog posts, interviews, etc.) about the absolute best companies.

My criteria

I personally gravitate towards organizations with an opinionated view of themselves. They've thought pretty deeply about their culture and they signal it, whether in the form of a well-crafted recruitment page, frequent blog posts, podcast appearances, or whatever. I'm also only featuring organizations that are “remote-first” or “remote-friendly”, not merely “remote-allowed” or those with firm geographic restrictions.
